|| GREENEHOUSEARTS || A Platform for Emerging Artists Based in Brooklyn NYC

GREENEHOUSEARTS holds space for creative engagement - open communication between artists of all mediums as they showcase their work, and by extension, their worldview. In doing so, we intend to facilitate dialogue, connection and the proliferation of explorative spaces, exhibitions that challenge thoughts surrounding some of the more difficult topics we often avoid.
